What is Join?

Join is a Kanban Project Management Tool. You and your team create tasks with the kanban board method. The stages of the work progress are visualized by the various columns of the board. Every task moves from the left to the right until it's done.

How to use it

1. Get ready to work
Sign in and get an account. Or use the guest account to look how the tool works.
2. Create the work environment
Add a task and watch it's work progress on the board. An urgent task is still open? The summary will give you the perfect overview!
3. Manage the progress
The Tasks can always be edited and adapted to the work progress. Stay updated and communicate with your team members.
